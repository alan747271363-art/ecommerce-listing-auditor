# Sample Audit Report

## Listing Audit

Score: 82/100

Margin: $6.19 contribution profit per order

Break-even ad spend: $11.19

## Diagnosis

The listing is understandable and has a clear product type, but it can do more
work for buyers who are comparing several similar blenders. The strongest next
step is to add proof points and more search-intent keywords without making the
title feel stuffed.

## Top Actions

1. Add 2-3 proof points such as warranty, review count, certification, or
   guarantee.
2. Add buyer-specific keywords that match search intent, such as travel blender,
   protein shake blender, or office smoothie cup if accurate.
3. Keep refund assumptions visible because they materially affect contribution
   profit.

## Rewrite Direction

Possible title:

Portable USB-C Blender, 500ml Rechargeable Smoothie Maker for Travel, Gym, and
Office

Possible bullet:

Blend smoothies, protein shakes, and fruit drinks anywhere with a rechargeable
USB-C motor, six-blade cup, and easy-rinse design for daily travel or office use.

## Risk Note

This is decision support, not a revenue guarantee. Listing changes should be
tested against real traffic, marketplace rules, and customer feedback.
