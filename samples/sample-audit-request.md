# Sample Audit Request

This sample shows the kind of information a seller can provide without exposing
private account data.

## Product

- Marketplace: Shopify store
- Category: Portable kitchen appliances
- Target market: United States
- Product: USB rechargeable portable blender

## Listing Copy

Title:

Portable Blender USB Rechargeable 500ml Smoothie Maker

Description:

Rechargeable portable blender with six blades, BPA-free cup, USB-C charging,
and easy cleaning for travel, gym, and office smoothies.

## Unit Economics

- Sale price: 29.99
- Landed product cost: 9.40
- Shipping or fulfillment cost: 3.20
- Marketplace/payment fee rate: 0.15
- Expected ad spend per order: 5.00
- Expected refund rate: 0.04

## Goal

Improve listing clarity and understand whether the current ad spend leaves
enough contribution profit per order.
