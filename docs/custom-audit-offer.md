# Custom Ecommerce Listing Audit

This repository is a free sample of a practical listing audit workflow. The CLI
can score a listing quickly, but many sellers need a human pass to connect the
score to product positioning, search intent, and margin pressure.

Paid custom audits can be requested through Ko-fi:

https://ko-fi.com/alan363

## Useful Paid Requests

- Review 1-5 product listings and return concrete rewrite suggestions
- Rewrite product titles and bullets for Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, or TikTok Shop
- Estimate break-even ad spend from price, cost, fees, shipping, and refunds
- Build a custom audit checklist for a repeatable product launch workflow
- Adapt the CLI scoring rules for a specific niche or marketplace

## What To Send

Share only non-sensitive information:

- Public listing link or listing text
- Product category and target market
- Sale price, landed cost, shipping cost, marketplace fee, and refund estimate
- Main business goal, such as more clicks, better conversion, or cleaner margins

Never send passwords, payment account access, customer data, ad account access,
or private order exports.

## Example Deliverable

A typical paid audit can include:

- Listing score and plain-language diagnosis
- Top 3 conversion risks
- Title rewrite options
- Bullet rewrite suggestions
- Margin and break-even ad spend summary
- Next-step checklist for testing changes

Audits are practical decision support, not a guarantee of revenue, ranking, ad
approval, or marketplace compliance.
